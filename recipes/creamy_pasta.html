Creamy Pasta Recipe

Description

A flavorful treat that can come in handy when craving something fast.

Ingredients

  • Milk (200 ml)
  • Spaghetti (200 g)
  • Ham (1 slice, 1 cm width)
  • Butter (15 g)
  • Minced garlic cloves (2-3 cloves)

Steps

  1. Melt butter in a nonstick pan over medium heat.
  2. Add chopped ham and fry until lightly browned.
  3. Pour in the milk and add minced garlic.
  4. Stir the mixture with a spatula every few seconds until the sauce begins to thicken.
  5. Toss in the boiled spaghetti. Stir-fry for another 2-3 minutes to coat evenly.
  6. Done.
