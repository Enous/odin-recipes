Scrambled Eggs Recipe

Description

A tasty breakfast dish that can be made in under five minutes.

Ingredients

  • Milk (1 tsp)
  • 2 eggs
  • Vegetable oil

Steps

  1. Add eggs and milk to a mixing vessel (like a bowl or a large cup) and stir until combined.
  2. Brush a nonstick pan with oil and heat it over medium heat.
  3. Pour in the mixture. Stir the eggs with a spatula every few seconds, scraping along the bottom and edges of the pan.
  4. When the eggs start looking like soft curds, remove the pan from the heat.
  5. Done.
